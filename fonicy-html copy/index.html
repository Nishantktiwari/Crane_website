About Us

Highlands Investment Limited is a trusted provider of heavy equipment rental services across Zambia and Africa. With years of experience in the industry, we specialize in supplying high-quality, well-maintained machinery for construction, industrial, and logistics projects. Our fleet includes a wide range of equipment, from cranes (18 Ton to 400 Ton) and excavators to dump trucks, forklifts, and low-bed trailers. Whether you need equipment for a short-term project or long-term use, we offer flexible rental solutions to meet your needs. At Highlands Investment Limited, we take pride in our commitment to safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our team of skilled professionals ensures that every piece of machinery is in excellent working condition, allowing you to complete your projects efficiently and on time. With our head office in Kitwe and a branch office in Chingola, we are well-positioned to serve clients across the region. Contact us today to find the right equipment for your project and experience the Highlands Investment Limited difference!
